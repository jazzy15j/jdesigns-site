Post 4 of 5 — Doubling Your Budget Got You Flagged — Screenshot this graphic
✦ Meta Ads Scaling

Your ads were finally working. So you doubled the budget.

🚩 What happened
$50 → $100
Overnight. Same week results started coming in.
Account flagged 2 days later.
Meta's fraud system saw an anomaly. It didn't know you were excited. It responded to the pattern.
Spend spike = threat signal.
✦ The rule
20–30%
Maximum budget increase per 7-day window.
Let the learning phase stabilize. Scale the budget — not the number of campaigns running.
$50/day → $500/day is a jump you have to earn incrementally.
Patience after good results = protected momentum.
The fastest way to lose momentum is to get restricted the week things start working.
