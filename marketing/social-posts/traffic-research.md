# JDesigns Organic Traffic Research Map

Generated 2026-06-29. This is the first search-backed traffic push for the current content library.

## Highest-intent problems to prioritize

### 1. Meta restriction and appeal problems

Search results and Meta documentation show users need help identifying the restricted layer, writing appeals, and avoiding repeated generic review requests.

Search language to use: Meta restricted ad account, request review, appeal rejected, Account Quality

### 2. Instagram to Facebook connection failures

Official Meta help and community posts repeatedly point to full control, Page connection, stuck connection states, and professional account linking issues.

Search language to use: Instagram won't connect to Facebook Page, cannot add Instagram to Business Portfolio

### 3. Pixel and event tracking errors

Meta's own help names duplicate pixel events as a common warning; current guides focus on inactive events, duplicate installs, and CAPI deduplication.

Search language to use: Meta pixel not working, duplicate events detected, Events Manager no activity

### 4. Business portfolio access confusion

People confuse asset access with portfolio full control; agency/client handoffs create high-intent troubleshooting searches.

Search language to use: Meta Business Portfolio access, full control, assign assets, agency owns ad account

### 5. Business verification friction

Verification warnings and rejected submissions create checklist-style searches tied to legal names, addresses, documents, and the right portfolio.

Search language to use: Meta business verification required, verification rejected


## 30-day posting structure

- Week 1: Meta access, restrictions, appeals, and ownership.
- Week 2: Instagram connection, Business Portfolio setup, and Page access.
- Week 3: Pixel, verification, campaign setup, funnel sequence, and scaling.
- Week 4: Instagram strategy, carousels, Trial Reels, Canva AI, and strategist-vs-tool education.

## CTA ladder

- Free first step: Meta Business Access Emergency Checklist.
- Paid self-serve: Meta Business Fix-It Vault.
- Direct help: Fiverr Meta Ecosystem Review or Meta Ads Strategy when the issue is complex or revenue-critical.

## Guardrails

- Do not promise reinstatement, ad approval, revenue, leads, or Meta outcomes.
- Use "review," "check," "guide," "next step," and "action plan."
- Avoid "I can fix this guaranteed" language.
