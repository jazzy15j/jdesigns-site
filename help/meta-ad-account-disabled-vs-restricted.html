Meta Restrictions

Meta Ad Account Disabled vs Restricted: What Is the Difference?

Meta restrictions can happen at the ad, ad account, user, Page, or business level. This guide helps you name the problem before acting.

Best for: User does not know what kind of restriction they have.

Signs this is your issue

Ads stopped delivering but the reason is unclear
One account is restricted but other assets still appear active
Business Support and Account Quality show different wording
You are not sure whether to appeal or fix setup first

What to do first

1
Identify the restricted layerCheck whether the warning names an ad, ad account, user account, Page, or business portfolio.
2
Screenshot the notice before clicking anythingYou need the exact wording, policy, date, and affected asset for a useful review.
3
Check whether review is availableSome cases have an appeal button, some require support, and some only show next-step instructions.
4
Match the response to the levelA Page restriction and an ad account restriction should not get the same appeal language.

What not to do

  • Do not appeal before reading the full notice.
  • Do not assume every restriction is caused by creative.
  • Do not promise yourself or a client that Meta will reverse it.
