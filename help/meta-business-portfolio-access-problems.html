Meta Access

Meta Business Portfolio Access Problems: Who Needs Full Control?

If Meta says another admin has to approve access, the issue is usually ownership, full control, or asset assignment.

Best for: User cannot add people, assign assets, or approve agency access.

Signs this is your issue

You can see the Page but cannot assign it
Meta says another admin must approve the change
The agency has asset access but not portfolio access
People, Pages, ad accounts, and Instagram live in different places

What to do first

1
Find the business portfolio that owns the assetsDo not start with the Page view. Start inside Business Settings and confirm the portfolio name and ID.
2
Check who has full controlTask access is not enough for ownership, admin changes, or some asset assignments.
3
Confirm the Page, Instagram, ad account, and pixel are in the same setupFragmented assets are a common cause of looping access problems.
4
Send one clean access requestAsk for the exact role and assets instead of asking the client to click around blindly.

What not to do

  • Do not create a second portfolio until ownership is confirmed.
  • Do not assume Page admin equals business portfolio control.
  • Do not accept partial access if the job requires setup changes.
