Install the Workshop

Open your terminal, navigate to the folder where you want to build your Business Brain, and run the install command for your operating system.

Mac / Linux
Terminal
curl -fsSL https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jazzy15j/build-your-business-brain/main/install.sh | bash

Run this from the folder where you want your Business Brain to live — or create a new folder first and run it there.

Windows (PowerShell)
PowerShell
irm https://raw.githubusercontent.com/jazzy15j/build-your-business-brain/main/install.ps1 | iex

Open PowerShell, navigate to your chosen folder, then paste and run the command above.

Your 7 Workshop Commands
/brain-start Begin the workshop — start here
/brain-setup Build your core business files step by step
/brain-prompts Build your personal prompt library
/brain-test Test that Claude knows your business correctly
/brain-done Wrap up and get your next steps
/brain-update Update your Brain as your business changes
/brain-help Get unstuck at any point in the workshop
